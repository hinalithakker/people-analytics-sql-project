# Key Findings

## Workforce Overview
- Total employees analyzed: 3000
- All three datasets matched successfully on `emp_id`

## Attrition
- Overall attrition rate: 12.9%
- Highest attrition:
  - Software Engineering: 17.4%
  - Production: 15.9%
- Lowest attrition:
  - Executive Office: 0.0%
  - Admin Offices: 1.3%

## Engagement
- Overall average engagement score: 2.94
- Overall average satisfaction score: 3.02
- Overall average work life balance score: 2.99

## Department Level Engagement
- Executive Office: 3.38
- IT/IS: 3.03
- Sales: 2.99
- Software Engineering: 2.97
- Admin Offices: 2.92
- Production: 2.91

## Main Insight
Attrition is not explained by engagement alone. Departments with similar engagement scores show very different attrition rates, suggesting that role structure, workload, and other operational factors may be stronger drivers of turnover.

## Training Insight
Training participation was uniform across all employees, so training did not provide meaningful differentiation for performance or retention analysis in this dataset.
